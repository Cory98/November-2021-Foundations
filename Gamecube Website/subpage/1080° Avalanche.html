1080° Avalanche

 1080° Avalanche is a snowboarding video game for the GameCube, developed by Nintendo's in-house development studio, Nintendo Software Technology, and published by Nintendo. It released in Europe on November 28, 2003, in North America on December 1, 2003, and in Japan on January 22, 2004. Avalanche is a sequel to the 1998 video game 1080° Snowboarding for the Nintendo 64.

In contrast to similar snowboarding games such as the SSX series, the game emphasizes racing rather than doing tricks. Gameplay can output in 480p and Dolby Pro Logic II and supports four players on one GameCube as well as LAN play with up to four GameCubes.
